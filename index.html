The Dude Named Ryan

If there were one word I would use to describe myself it would be evolving. Over the years there have been many interactions and experiences in my life, each having helped me to grow and to gain insight into how to live a more fulfilling and rewarding life. Every experience, whether good or bad, has a lesson to be learned. I try not to take myself too seriously, and I have never been great at any one thing, but I feel as if I do many things well. I care about others and doing what is right, and try my best to work through every barrier preventing progress; if there is a way I will find it. I feel that I am a work in progress, and as soon as I stop being self-critical, will also be the day I stop learning.
